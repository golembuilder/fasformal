What is FAS Formal?

FAS Formal is a yearly student formal organized and run by students in the Faculty of Applied Science at SFU. Each year, over 150 undergraduate students, staff, faculty, and alumni get dressed up and come together to eat, drink, socialize, and dance the night away!

Celebrating Us!

This year's theme is all about celebrating who we are as a faculty, what our individual student societies have accomplished, and what we all bring to SFU. We have many talented, passionate, and dedicated students, staff, and faculty, and we're proud to celebrate their accomplishments.

Sponsorship

FAS Formal would not be possible without the help of our partners at Simon Fraser University and the Simon Fraser Student Student, as well as sponsors like you!

Our total budget costs this year is $18,000 with the majority of costs going to food, venue, security, and entertainment.

This year we have had enormous support from the

  • SFU Faculty of Applied Science Dean's Office, Simon Fraser University
  • Simon Fraser Student Society
  • Engineering Science Student Society
  • Mechatronics Systems Engineering Student Society
  • Software Systems Student Society
  • Computing Science Student Society
who have in total contributed $7500. Their generous support, both with financial and volunteer contributions, have allowed us to subsidize the ticket cost of student attendees by 50%

If you choose to sponsor us, your contribution will go towards venue, catering, and supporting undergraduate students who are providing our entertainment and photography services. Subsidizing these costs costs allows us to lower the costs on students further. For every $1000 we raise, that lowers student ticket prices by $5.
